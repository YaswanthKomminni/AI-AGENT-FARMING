GOVERNMENT SCHEMES AND SUBSIDIES FOR FARMERS — INDIA
Source: Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, Government of India

=== CENTRAL GOVERNMENT SCHEMES ===

1. PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)
   Benefit: ₹6,000/year in 3 installments of ₹2,000 each
   Eligibility:
   - Small and marginal farmers with up to 2 hectares cultivable land
   - Indian citizen with valid Aadhaar
   - Not a government employee, income taxpayer, or pensioner
   Exclusions: Institutional land holders, professionals, retired government officials
   How to apply: pmkisan.gov.in; online or through CSC
   Required documents: Aadhaar, land records (Khasra/Khatoni), bank account
   Status check: pmkisan.gov.in/Beneficiarystatus.aspx

2. PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana) — CROP INSURANCE
   Purpose: Protect farmers from financial losses due to natural disasters, pests, diseases
   Premium paid by farmer:
   - Kharif crops: 2% of sum insured
   - Rabi crops: 1.5% of sum insured
   - Horticulture/Annual commercial crops: 5% of sum insured
   Government pays the balance premium
   Coverage:
   - Pre-sowing to post-harvest losses
   - Cyclone, flood, drought, hailstorm, landslide
   - Localized calamities (hailstorm, landslide)
   - Post-harvest (14 days) losses due to cyclone/unseasonal rain
   How to apply:
   - Through bank (for loanee farmers — mandatory)
   - Non-loanee: Any bank, CSC, or pmfby.gov.in
   Deadline: Within 2 weeks of crop sowing
   Claim: Notify within 72 hours of damage via crop insurance app or 14447 helpline

3. KCC (Kisan Credit Card)
   Purpose: Short-term credit for crop production and maintenance
   Interest rate: 4% (with timely repayment; 7% otherwise)
   Credit limit: Up to ₹3 lakh without collateral
   For: Crop loans, post-harvest expenses, maintenance of farm assets
   Eligibility: All farmers including sharecroppers and tenant farmers
   Apply at: Any nationalized bank, cooperative bank, regional rural bank (RRB)
   Documents: Aadhaar, land records, passport photo, bank account

4. PMKSY (Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana)
   Components:
   a) Per Drop More Crop: Drip/sprinkler irrigation
      Subsidy: 40–90% on equipment (higher for SC/ST/small farmers)
   b) Watershed Development
   c) Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Programme (AIBP)
   Apply at: District Agriculture/Horticulture Office
   Online: pmksy.gov.in

5. PKVY (Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana)
   Purpose: Promote organic farming
   Benefit: ₹50,000/ha for 3 years (50,000 = ₹31,000 for inputs + ₹8,800 value addition + ₹3,200 cluster formation)
   How it works: Farmers form clusters of 20–50 hectares; group organic farming
   Apply at: KVK (Krishi Vigyan Kendra) or Block Agriculture Office

6. SMAM (Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization)
   Subsidy on:
   - Tractors: 25–50% (SC/ST get 50%)
   - Power tillers: 40–50%
   - Combine harvesters: 25–40%
   - Sprayers, seeders, planters: 40–50%
   Apply: State Agriculture Department portal or district office

7. Soil Health Card (SHC) Scheme
   Benefit: Free soil testing every 2 years; personalized fertilizer recommendations
   Coverage: All 14 crore farm holdings
   Apply: Nearest Soil Testing Lab or Agriculture Department
   Online: soilhealth.dac.gov.in

8. e-NAM (National Agriculture Market)
   Purpose: Pan-India electronic trading platform for agricultural produce
   Benefits: Transparent price discovery; higher prices for farmers; reduced middlemen
   Registered mandis: 1,260+ APMCs across 18 states
   Apply: enam.gov.in or nearest APMC mandi

=== STATE-SPECIFIC SCHEMES ===

Maharashtra
- Mahatma Phule Shetkari Karjamukti Yojana: Farm loan waiver
- Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjivany Project
- Raytu Bandhu scheme (Telangana): ₹5,000/acre/season

Telangana / Andhra Pradesh
- Rythu Bandhu: ₹5,000/acre/crop season investment support
- YSR Free Crop Insurance (AP)

Punjab / Haryana
- Crop diversification scheme (incentive to shift from paddy)
- Kisan Karj Mafi Yojana

Karnataka
- Krishi Bhagya scheme: Irrigation support
- Raita Shakthi Scheme

Tamil Nadu
- Chief Minister's Comprehensive Crop Insurance
- Farm Mechanization Scheme (50% subsidy)

=== CREDIT AND LOANS ===

Nabard (National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development)
- KCC loans through cooperatives and RRBs
- Rural Infrastructure Development Fund (RIDF)
- Micro-credit for SHGs

NABARD Farmer Producer Organization (FPO) scheme
- Equity grant up to ₹15 lakh per FPO
- Credit guarantee fund
