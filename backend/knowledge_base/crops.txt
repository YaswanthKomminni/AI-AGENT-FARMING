CROP SELECTION AND CULTIVATION GUIDE — INDIA
Source: ICAR (Indian Council of Agricultural Research) / State Agriculture Universities

=== KHARIF CROPS (June–October) ===

RICE (Paddy)
- Best soil: Alluvial, clay, waterlogged
- Water requirement: 1200–2000 mm/season
- Temperature: 20–35°C
- Key varieties: Swarna, MTU-1010, Pusa Basmati 1121, HMT Sona
- Seed rate: 20–25 kg/ha (transplanted); 60–80 kg/ha (direct seeded)
- Spacing: 20x15 cm
- Duration: 105–150 days depending on variety
- Key states: WB, UP, Punjab, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Odisha
- Yield potential: 4–8 ton/ha

MAIZE
- Best soil: Sandy loam to loam, well-drained
- Water: 500–800 mm
- Temperature: 18–27°C
- Key varieties: HQPM-1, Pioneer 30V92, Dekalb 9144
- Seed rate: 20–25 kg/ha
- Spacing: 60x25 cm
- Duration: 80–110 days
- Yield: 5–8 ton/ha

COTTON
- Best soil: Black cotton soil (vertisol), red loam
- Water: 700–1200 mm
- Temperature: 25–35°C
- Key varieties: Bt Cotton hybrids (Bollgard II), Suraj, LRA 5166
- Seed rate: 1–1.5 kg/ha (Bt hybrids)
- Spacing: 90x60 cm
- Duration: 170–210 days
- Yield: 1.5–3.5 ton/ha (seed cotton)

SOYBEAN
- Best soil: Black, alluvial
- Water: 450–700 mm
- Temperature: 26–30°C
- Key varieties: JS 93-05, MACS 450, Phule Kalyani
- Seed rate: 65–70 kg/ha
- Spacing: 45x5 cm
- Duration: 90–110 days
- Yield: 1.5–3 ton/ha

GROUNDNUT
- Best soil: Sandy loam, red laterite
- Water: 500–700 mm
- Temperature: 25–30°C
- Key varieties: TG-37A, Kadiri-5, Girnar-2
- Seed rate: 80–100 kg/ha
- Duration: 90–125 days
- Yield: 2–4 ton/ha

BAJRA (Pearl Millet)
- Best soil: Sandy loam, red soil — drought tolerant
- Water: 300–400 mm
- Temperature: 27–35°C
- Key varieties: HHB 67, GHB 538, Rajshree
- Seed rate: 4–5 kg/ha
- Duration: 65–90 days
- Yield: 2–3.5 ton/ha

ARHAR (Pigeon Pea)
- Best soil: Loam, clay loam
- Water: 600–900 mm
- Temperature: 18–30°C
- Key varieties: ICPL 87119, Asha, Rajiv Lochan
- Duration: 150–200 days
- Yield: 1.5–2.5 ton/ha

=== RABI CROPS (November–March) ===

WHEAT
- Best soil: Loam, clay loam, alluvial
- Water: 400–500 mm (4–6 irrigations)
- Temperature: 10–25°C
- Key varieties: HD-2967, HD-3086, GW-496, PBW-550
- Seed rate: 100–125 kg/ha
- Spacing: Row spacing 22–25 cm
- Duration: 120–150 days
- Yield: 4–6 ton/ha

MUSTARD / RAPESEED
- Best soil: Loam to sandy loam
- Water: 2–3 irrigations
- Temperature: 10–25°C
- Key varieties: Pusa Bold, Kranti, Varuna
- Seed rate: 5–6 kg/ha
- Duration: 90–130 days
- Yield: 1.5–2.5 ton/ha

CHICKPEA (Gram)
- Best soil: Sandy loam to loam
- Water: Mostly rainfed; 1 irrigation at flowering
- Temperature: 10–25°C
- Key varieties: GNG-1581, JG-11, Pusa 362
- Seed rate: 70–80 kg/ha
- Duration: 95–120 days
- Yield: 1.5–2.5 ton/ha

BARLEY
- Best soil: Loam, tolerates alkaline soil
- Water: 2–3 irrigations
- Temperature: 10–20°C
- Key varieties: DWRB-91, K-551, Vijaya
- Duration: 100–120 days
- Yield: 2.5–4 ton/ha

=== ZAID / SUMMER CROPS ===

WATERMELON
- Water: 400–600 mm
- Temperature: 25–35°C
- Duration: 60–80 days
- Yield: 20–40 ton/ha

CUCUMBER
- Water: 300–500 mm
- Temperature: 20–30°C
- Duration: 50–60 days
- Yield: 15–25 ton/ha

MOONG (Green Gram — Summer)
- Water: 250–400 mm
- Temperature: 25–35°C
- Duration: 60–70 days
- Yield: 0.8–1.2 ton/ha

=== SOIL-CROP MATCHING ===

Alluvial soils: Rice, Wheat, Sugarcane, Maize, Vegetables
Black cotton soils: Cotton, Soybean, Sorghum, Wheat, Chickpea
Red soils: Groundnut, Millets, Pulses, Vegetables
Laterite soils: Tea, Coffee, Cashew, Rice, Vegetables
Sandy soils: Pearl Millet, Cluster Bean, Groundnut, Watermelon
Saline/Alkaline: Barley, Rice (salt tolerant varieties), Dhaincha (green manure)
